for on line communications hosting to the internet of things

Welcome!

As the name implies, we communicate from device to device and host the information, an example, we are digitizing soil and everything that goes with it. Our goal is to utilize the data gathered by devices with sensors and apply them to your real live work flow, in a way that instead of working for the system let the system work for you.

We all know that watering plants and gathering data now can be done with simple devices called (IoT) Internet of Things. Yet I have not seen one that works for you, I mean, yes you can see the data, turn on and off, but a system that can be triggered and implement AI on such triggers that I have not seen. The idea is to represent reality with today's technology with today's field data gathering and workforce management. By creating sensors and preferences that work for you.

We start with an Open source platform dedicated to field of agriculture. Providing a consule to create and monitor good compost soil, Growing, and maintenance of your plants, and farm equipment. Our goal is to provide a web base application where all your data can be utilized in your favor. Let the machine do your automated tasks and provide you with useful data that will be implemented through all API connections with other Internet of Things Providers and Services. This will be your home base preference manager for all your Internet of Things and Field Work managing web site.

With the help of Soil FoodWeb Alliance we are know able to provide a source where a professional may view and post opinions and recommendations on your compost pile and turning cycles.

With the implementation of the Internet of Things, field work and field sensors can integrate as a service, providing a place where the data gathered will work for you. Resources for decision making and automatization of desired events.

Sensors can be implemented no matter your service provider, as long as they have API services.

Certainly you have heard of Internet of Things, as a big topic and that it is the future. What is Internet of Things and wy should I use it. First lets get to basics, the meaning of Internet of Things is that the device can send data and receive data, like us for example. We can send data by voice, and receive data by voice. Well this devices can do the same, but they do it through the Internet. Meaning that your device can talk to you or your smart phone, as long as you both are connected to the Internet, from anywhere.

More than that, you can automate the system to report or send SMS or email to the desired person or person in charge of that specific event. By communicating the event to the desired person, results can be achieved.

Sensor and Devices

We are working on an example where the device will provide data, in this instance temperature and humidity from a pile of compost. Digital Soil, our web based system, will store that data and provide the information in various manners in order to process and view the information with graphics and specs.
